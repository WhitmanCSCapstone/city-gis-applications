Why Choose Whitman?

Challenging. Supportive. Open-minded.

You're driven, passionate and curious. Whether in the classroom or studying out in the field, you're looking for intellectual challenges. You want a close-knit, supportive community, stellar academics and a campus that's as open-minded as it is beautiful. At Whitman, you'll investigate your academic interests, find your passions and make intellectual and personal connections that last a lifetime.
