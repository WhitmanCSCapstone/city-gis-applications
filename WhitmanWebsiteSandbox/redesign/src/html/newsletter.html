Whitman toasts Chuck Cleveland


Chuck Cleveland receives a hug from his wife, Pat Sorenson '86, at his recent retirement celebration.

Whitman staff, faculty, students and alumni came together last Monday to honor Vice President of Student Affairs/Dean of Students Chuck Cleveland, who retired this year after 36 years of service to the college. Cleveland began working at Whitman as assistant director/support services in 1981, became acting director of housing in 1984, assumed the title of director of housing six months later in 1985, rose to dean of students in 1994 and became vice president for student affairs in 2015.

Colleagues congratulated him on his long and storied career and presented him with a book of remembrances. A tribute video by film and media studies major Tywin Kelly '18 captured additional fond sentiments, as did a performance of Tim McGraw's 2015 hit single "Humble and Kind" by residence life staff. The Fountain now on reduced schedule

This week's Fountain will be the last weekly issue until the start of the fall 2017 semester. The reduced summer schedule will include one issue of The Fountain published on the last Monday of the month for June and July. Submissions will still be accepted throughout the summer. Please submit at least three business days before anticipated publication. The Fountain returns to a weekly publication on Monday, Aug. 21. 

To submit, email thefountain@whitman.edu or fill out the News Tips form at the bottom of the page. 

OP to offer discounts on merchandise, weekend rentals

In honor of Staff Recognition Day next Friday, June 2, the Outdoor Program Rental Shop will offer all Whitman staff and faculty 20 percent off retail merchandise, a $20 coupon or 50 percent off of a weekend rental for the weekend of June 2-5 (minor exclusions apply for raft rentals). The shop will be open from the close of the staff breakfast until 1:30 p.m. that day for people to rent gear.

Questions? Contact Outdoor Program Rental Shop Manager Lish Riley.
